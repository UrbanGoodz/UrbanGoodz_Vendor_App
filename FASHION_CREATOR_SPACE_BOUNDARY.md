# Urban Goodz Creator Commerce & Sizing Privacy Boundary

## Status
- **Ready**: Creator Commerce contains previews of fashion content, educational fit tips, and public drops while protecting sizing profile photos.
- **Preview-only**: Navigation path links between sizing dashboards and shopping feeds are operational.
- **Blocked**: Admin moderation control blocks and automated content filters to scan user profiles.

---

## 1. Mapped Public Previews
* **Fashion Drops**: Public outfit catalogs and collections promoted by local creators.
* **Measurement Education**: Public instructions, video guides, and preparation tips showing posture calibration.
* **Tailor Showcase**: Public galleries showcasing designer catalogs and custom garments.

---

## 2. Protected Private Areas
* **Sizing Profiles**: Manual body dimensions (waist, sleeve, chest, hips) are kept private.
* **Posture Photos**: Front and side standing photos are used strictly by the target tailor and are blocked from public creator feeds.

---

## 3. Production Blockers
- Platform moderation tools to report or purge inappropriate public fashion uploads.
- Explicit customer consent toggle options before publishing any before/after comparison examples.
